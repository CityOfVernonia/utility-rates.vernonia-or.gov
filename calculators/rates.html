Water Budget

The water budget is comprised of two components. Payroll includes wages, health care costs, retirement and other employee related expenses. Operations and Maintenance (O & M) is the amount budgeted to operate and maintain the water system in the coming year.

Projected + is any amount of revenue from the previous fiscal year above expenditures, which is not required as part of next year's budget. These funds result from additional revenue from water system growth, fiscal responsibilty by the City, and over the counter reciepts such as late fees. City Council and the Budget Committee may decide on some combination of funding additional projects in the budget, adding to reserve funds (see below), and/or refunding the ratepayer. Any amount used to calculate next year's rates reduces the net amount of revenue required to be generated by the base and consumptive rates.
